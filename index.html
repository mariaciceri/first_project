Climbing History

Climbing as a sport started in the beginning of the 19th century with single-pitch climbing. Bouldering and multi-pitch climbing came a little bit later, around 1930.

In the 2020 Tokya Olympic Games, sport-climbing had its debut with 3 different categories: boulder, lead and speed climbing. Athletes had to complete in all three categories, which changed in the 2024 Paris Olympics Games, when speed climb got separeted from the othe two modalities.

There are many diffenret grades for the climbing routes, but the most common ones in Europe are French font-grade and American V system.

About Us

We are a group of 11 people passionate about climbing and alpinism. Each one of us started this journey in a different way but we met all because of this amazing and exciting sport.

We decided to provide help for others that are starting this new and brilliant sport or someone that wants to step up their climbing game. So we created Climbing Rox to provide advice on different topics, share our own adventures to inspire others and answer any specific questions you may have (write to us on Get Help).

Go to our Advice section and learn more! Happy climbing!
